Conic Blitz

The following is a fast-paced game wherein the player solves a series of problems involving conic sections and important measures as much as possible under a grand time of 60 seconds.

By going to the this section, the players must be knowledgeable on how to calculate the measures given in the following table.

Parabola Ellipse Hyperbola
Latus Rectum Length Latus Rectum Length Latus Rectum Length
Focus Coordinates Foci Coordinates Foci Coordinates
Vertex Coordinates Center Coordinates Center Coordinates
Eccentricity Eccentricity Eccentricity

The question type is multiple choice. Choose the best answer among the given choices. Each correct answer will merit the player 2 additional seconds of answering while each incorrect answer will demerit the player 1 second of answering.
